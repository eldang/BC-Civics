Provincial Electoral Districts - Electoral Boundaries Redistribution, 2023 For use in the administration of provincial elections. A geographic area defined in the Electoral Districts Act that is represented in the Legislative Assembly by a single MLA. Electoral districts are also known as constituencies or ridings. (Constitution Act, s. 18) The 2023 Electoral Boundaries Commission divided B.C. into 93 electoral districts.
